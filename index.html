    Thanks for visiting Beads by NBJ!

    I'm Noa Fisher-Jerryman, the founder and CEO of Beads by NBJ. My hand-crafted bracelets are made with a variety of beads types. I specifically enjoy the texture of the lava bead! Each bead has so much character...they have their own individual story to tell. It is my ultimate goal to provide a creative and comfortable product for everyday wear. As a child, I enjoyed making crafts, specifically bracelets. As I got older, I continued to make bracelets, discovering new materials along the way. This passion has led me to creating an unique bracelet line of my own.
